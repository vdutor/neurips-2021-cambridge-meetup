Welcome

This in-person NeurIPS meetup aims to bring together people from the greater Cambridge area (U.K.) for an opportunity to meet and discuss machine learning research presented at NeurIPS 2021. We also want to provide an opportunity for researchers to promote their work to a wider audience, and engage in technical conversations in a positive environment.

The one-day meetup will host a poster session for accepted NeurIPS papers, stream a NeurIPS talk (tutorial and/or best paper award), and a reading group around a few selected NeurIPS papers. The day will be concluded by a networking event with some food and drinks.
